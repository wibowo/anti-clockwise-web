Knower of Things
A companion app for Things in Rings
Things in Rings is better when nobody has to be the deck. Knower of Things shuffles for you and lends a second brain when a card refuses to be categorized.
◆Card randomizer — draw a fresh card in one tap, no setup, no repeats.
◆Ask the bot — stuck on a Thing? The bot suggests which ring it might belong in.
◆Table-friendly — big type, quick taps, designed to be passed around.
